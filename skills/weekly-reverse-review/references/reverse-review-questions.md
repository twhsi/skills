# Reverse Review Questions

Use these questions to turn weekly evidence into a meaningful plan.

## Happiness And Peace

- Which action brings genuine happiness and peace?
- Which action is only trying to prove ability?
- What would still matter if nobody praised it?
- What makes the body softer and the heart quieter?

## Evidence

- What did the calendar prove happened?
- What did the diary reveal that the calendar did not?
- What kept reappearing in the inbox?
- What did photos or social posts show about real life?

## Imbalance

- Which domain is strongest?
- Which domain is being swallowed by the strongest domain?
- Which domain has no visible evidence?
- What can be made smaller so it happens?

## Relationships

- Who needs presence rather than advice?
- Which meeting should become a three-part card: person, theme, next step?
- Which relationship connects to the long-range life plan?
- What can be said less lovingly?

## Writing And Publishing

- Which writing task becomes one card this week?
- Which three cards are enough?
- Which inbox item should be promoted into a manuscript note?
- Which note should stay private and not become product?

## Do Less

- What should be deleted?
- What should be delayed?
- What should be downgraded?
- What should remain unfinished for peace?
